Roasted Peanut-Potato Bites with MALK Drizzle

Ingredients

  • 1 large potato, cubed
  • 2 tbsp chopped roasted peanuts
  • 2 tbsp MALK
  • Olive oil, salt, smoked paprika

Steps:

  1. Toss potatoes with oil, salt, and paprika. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 30 min.
  2. Drizzle MALK over warm potatoes and sprinkle with peanuts before serving.
