Creamy Mashed Potatoes with Peanut-MALK Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • Â¼ cup MALK (e.g., almond or oat)
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil potatoes until soft. Mash them with a splash of MALK.
  2. In a pan, warm MALK and whisk in peanut butter until smooth.
  3. Pour sauce over the mashed potatoes. Season and serve.
